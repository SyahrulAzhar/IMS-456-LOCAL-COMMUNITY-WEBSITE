Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) Jenderam Hilir

Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) Jenderam Hilir is a primary school that is located in Jenderam Hilir. The school's location is right next to Masjid As-Syakirin, which allowed students and teachers there the access to pray during school's time.

The school was built by the locals of Jenderam Hilir, where it was built by mutual cooperations between the locals of Jenderam Hilir on the year of 1945. On the year 1960, the school received its new building and officially launched by Dato' Abu Bakar Baginda, who is the Selangor's MB at that time.

In 1969, the school got another two new buildings which is Block A for Office, Teacher's Room, some claasrooms, and Computer's Lab while Block B for classrooms and Schhol's Resource Center. Next, in the year 1998, the school received another new three-story building to accomodate more students in the school. Finally, the school received two one-story buildings to placed Standard 1, 2, and 3 classrooms and "Bilik Pemulihan:.


Sekolah Rendah Agama (SRA) Jenderam Hilir

Sekolah Rendah Agama (SRA) Jenderam Hilir is a Islamic primary school that is located in Jenderam Hilir. The school is also located near Masjid As-Syakirin. Not many official history were known about this school.

While there aren't any official info, there are some rumors among the locals in Jenderam Hilir said that the area where the school is was once a public soccer field. The locals frequently used the field to held many kinds of events such as soccer matches and community events.

However, the "older generations" (as the locals called them) felt like the field should be used for more useful functions. Therefore, the "older generations" proposed to have a school that teach specifically Islamic subjects and the proposal was accepted. Hence, the school was built while the public soccer field were moved elsewhere, although the field also not survived as a result of the usage of the field as sand mine.


Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) Dengkil

Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) Dengkil is a primary school that is located in Pekan Dengkil. While the school is categorized as rural school, the bustling environment of Pekan Dengkil made it seems not like it.

Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) Dengkil was established in 1926, where the its first buildin were built using woods with mutual cooperations of the locals. After that, two new buildings were built in 1982 and 1983. While in 1997, another new building were built as well as student's hostel to accomodate students whose parents are low-income.

In 2015, one massive fire destroyed a whole one-story block of the school. Even so, the fire didn't took any lives as the fire happened when students were not at school.


Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan (SMK) Dengkil

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan (SMK) Dengkil is a secondary school that is located in Pekan Dengkil. The school is quite near to the busy road of Pekan Dengkil, which may make it hard for students to be dropped of or taken back to home.

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan (SMK) Dengkil was established in January 1967, and officially launched on 11 January 1970 by Dato' Ahmad Razali Bin Mohd Ali, the Selangor's MB at that time. At first, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan (SMK) Dengkil started its operation as "sekolah tumpang" in Sekolah Kebangsaan (SK) Dengkil

Currently, the school have 6 building blocks, 6 "Kemahiran Hidup" workshops, a Bestari Computer's Lab, a Simulation Room, 6 teacher's houses, a canteen and "surau".


Sekolah Rendah Agama (SRA) Dengkil

Sekolah Rendah Agama (SRA) Dengkil is a Islamic primary school that is located in Pekan Dengkil. The school main entrance is quite hidded from plain view, so it may make the visitors having hard time to fins the school's entrance.

Even so, in 2021, during the country's latest flash flood, the school become a relief center or PPS (short form of Pusat Pemindahan Sementara) for the flood victims in the area.

University College of Aviation Malaysia (UniCAM)

University College of Aviation Malaysia (UniCAM) is a university specialize in aviation field. The university is located in Jenderam Hilir and near Masjid As-Syakirin. The university was estabished as Aviation Management College on 21 May 2007 before it was upgraded to Univeristy College of Aviation Malaysia (UniCAM) on 24 November 2020. The university is the first and oldest aviation university college in Malaysia's history.

As the name suggests, the university provide learning courses related to aviation such as Airline Cabin Crew Training, Aviation Security Awareness, Passenger Ground Services, and Travel and Tourism Consultant.


Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Dengkil

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Dengkil is a public university branch from Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). The university is currently located in Pekan Dengkil.

The university started the constructions of its buidings on November 2012 and finished its constructions on November 2015, make it the newest branch of UiTM campus. This university branch is different than others as this is the only campus of all UiTM campuses that offered Foundation Level, which made the campus known as "Pusat Asasi Universiti Teknologi MARA".
