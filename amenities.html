Shell Petrol Station, Jenderam Hilir

This petrol station is located near the main road to exit Jenderam Hilir. The petrol station started its construction on the end of 2019 and finished on the end of 2021.

The construction of the petrol station allows locals from Jenderam Hilir to refuel their transportation without searching much farther petrol station such as Pekan Dengkil and Teras Jernang.


Nurseri Petani Kota

Nurseri Petani Kota is a wholesale plant nursery that is located near Jenderam Hilir. The nursery provided many options of type of plants that can choose from. Also, the nursery also provided good quality soil compositions, composts, and fertilizers for plants. In addition, the nursery also provide many designs of vases and pots for the plants.

Usually, local plant lovers in Jenderam Hilir will visit the nursery to find new plants to grow at their homes along with buying multiple designs, shapes, and size of vases and pots and high quality composts and fertilizers.


Corner Print & Copy

Corner Print & Copy is a local print and photocopy service with several branches in Malaysia and one of them is located in Jenderam Hilir. The shop is located right next to Surau Al-Hidayah.

While the shop is relatively small since the shop owner built the shop himself, the shop does not stop from receiving customers. This is because the price of print and photography services of 2.5 cents made it one of the popular print and photocopy services in Jenderam Hilir.


Klinik AL Raffah Nur

Klinik Al Raffah Nur is a clinic that are located in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The clinic was established in the location in 2019 and has been the main choice for the locals in Jenderam Hilir to do their medical check-up.

While the clinic specialize in treating women and children, there are still multiple services provided by the clinic such as ultrasound scan, nebulizer treatment, and jaundice preliminary test. As for their operation hour, the clinic operated from 8:00 am - 10:00 pm.


Pasar Mini Jenderam Jaya

Pasar Mini Jenderam Jaya is grocery store located in Wima Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The store is one of the first businesses that established right after the completion of the Wisma construction.

The store provided everyday groceries to the locals of Jenderam Hilir such as fishes, chickens, meats, vegetables, rices, spices, and many more. The store is more popular to the locals as the store products' price are cheaper compared to others.


IK Easy Laundry

IK Easy Laundry is a self-service laundry shop that is located in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The laundry shop is also one of the first businesses opened right after the completion of the Wisma construction.

The laundry shop usually visited by students from nearby University College of Aviation Malaysia (UniCAM) and Kolej Komuniti Cawangan Sepang, but locals also visit to use the cheap laudry services provided by the shop.


Kedai Serbaneka

Kedai Serbaneka is a convenient store that is also located in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The convenient store provide stationeries such as pens, papers, pencils, and erasers. The store also sell everyday tools such as water buckets, water dippers, and plastic stools.

While the store mainly sell products, the store also provide print and photocopy services, although not as the main services of the store.


Kedai Gunting Rambut Mesra

Kedai Gunting Rambut Mesra is barber shop located in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The barber was established recently on 2019.Since then, the barber shop become one of that main choice for the locals, especially men, to get their hair done.

During the heights of COVID-19, the shop was closed for the entirety of the MCO. However, the shop managed to survive during MCO period and currently operate from 10:00 am - 10:00 pm.


A Two Hardware

A Two Hardware is a hardware shop located in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The shop is also one of the businesses established right after the completion of the Wisma construction.

The hardware shop provide a variety of metal tools, paints, pipe and ducts, electrical appliances and hardwares, home tools, and many more hardwares. The shop become locals' one stop centre for hardwares and tools. The shop currently operates from 9:00 am - 7:00 pm.


Farmasi LH Plus

Farmasi LH Plus is pharmacy that currently have three branches in Malaysia, one of it is located in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah, Jenderam Hilir. The pharmacy provide the locals with pharmaceuticals, health and beauty products, and prescription medicines.

Right now, the pharmacy operates everyday from 9:00 am - 9:00 pm. As the name suggests, locals usually visited the pharmacy if they want to re-supply their medications, food supplements, beauty products, medical tools and hardwares, and many more medical related products.


J&T Express Jenderam Hilir

J&T Express Jenderam Hilir is courier service business that is established in Wisma Aziz dan Rahmah. The courier services business just recently been established in the location at the end of 2021.

Before the establishment of the business in Jenderam Hilir, locals usually have to travel to Pekan Dengkil to access J&T Express services. Now, locals can easily go to this location as the courier services now located nearer to their homes.


Pasar Awam Dengkil

Pasar Awam Dengkil is a wet market that is located in Pekan Dengkil. The market opening hours started from 6:00 am - 12:00 pm. The markey accomodates many sellers and vendors that sells groceries such as fishes, chickens, meats, and vegetables.

While there are many many grocery stores that locals can visit, Pasar Awam Dengkil still one of the main groceries supplier for locals in Jenderam Hilir and Dengkil. As they usually open early in the morning, many of their produces are fresh from the suppliers. Also, there's also a night market located right next to the market's building where the opening hours started from 6:00 pm - 10:00 pm every Saturday.
