Historical Places


Sri Banting Dredge

Sri Banting Dredge is an old, abandoned tin dredge that was used to mine the tin ores during the old days of Malaya. Now, the dredge laid abandoned new ELITE Highway near Dengkil, Selangor.

The 4,500 tons dredge was last used and operated in 1977. The dredge also was the last ever dredge that exist in Selangor.


Kampung Orang Asli Bukit Dugang

Kampung Orang Asli Bukit Dugang is an "Orang Asli" village near Jenderam Hilir. The village was formed a long time ago but have been officially has its existence recorded around 2010.

There are few facilities located inside the village such as Surau Al-Falah Kg. Orang Asli Bukit Dugang and Tabika Kampung Orang Asli Bukit Dugang.
