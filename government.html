Balai Polis Dengkil

Balai Polis Dengkil is a police station that is located in the middle of Pekan Dengkil. While it is a small station, the police station have served the community inceptions in 1926.

The police original site still used untill today, while the police station building itself have received many changes along the way as to make the police station served the community better as well as accomodate more polices.


Pejabat Pos Dengkil

Pejabat Pos Dengkil is a post office that is located in Pekan Dengkil. The post office is also located near SRA Dengkil.

The post office started its operations 5 April 1982 and still serving the community till this day even in today's modern communications age.


Loji Rawatan Air Sungai Semenyih 2

Loji Rawatan Air Sungai Semenyih 2 is water treatment plant that is located in Jenderam Hilir. The secluded location of the water treatment plant made it easier for plant to operate easier without any interference from any bustling busy towns and city.

Firstly established in 2018, the plant has served many of Selangor districts, including Jenderam Hilir in providing clean water to the people living on those districts.


Jabatan Kastam Diraja Malaysia Dengkil

Jabatan Kastam Diraja Malaysia Dengkil is a customs department branch located in Pekan Dengkil. The customs department here managed all the customs related stuff in Dengkil and its surrounding area.


Lembaga Perlesenan Tenaga Atom

Lembaga Perlesenan Tenaga Atom is a government institution that is located in Jenderam Hilir. The institution was established in April 1984 where Malaysia's Parliament have approved the "Akta Perlesenan Tenaga Atom" which also known as Act 304. The government institution is specialized in controlling, checking, and enforcing any atomic energy activity in Malaysia.


Agensi Nuklear Malaysia

Agensi Nuklear Malaysia, Kompleks Jalan Dengkil is a government agency located in Jenderam Hilir. While this building here is not the main office building (the main office building is nearby), the agency itself still function in researching the usage of atomic energy in Malaysia.

Firstly known as Unit Tenaga Nuklear (UTN) in 1983, the agency changed its name to Institut Penyelidikan Teknologi Nuklear Malaysia (MINT) in August 1994. However, the agency again changed its name to its current name, Agensi Nuklear Malaysia in April 2005.
