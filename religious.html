Religious Places


Surau Al-Muhajirin

Surau Al-Muhajirin is one of the first "surau" established in Jenderam Hilir, being built in 1980s. The surau there become a palce where Muslim community in Kampung Jenderam Hilir gathered to pray and held many community events

Currently, the surau is having its renovation in order to accomodate more people during prayers as well as increasing the comfort in the surau.


Surau Al-Hidayah

Surau Al-Hidayah is an old surau located in Kamoung Jenderam Hilir. Located near the newly built Shell petrol station. This provided travellers with access of comfortable prayer.

While it is a surau, the building style is like normal village house, a bit small to accomodate many people in one time. However, few renovations here and there allows the surau to still be one of the suraus that locals used to pray in Jenderam Hilir.


Masjid As-Syakirin

Masjid As-Syakirin is a mosque located in Kampung Jenderam Hilir. Statrted its construction in 2009 and finished its contructions near the end of 2010.

At the time, the mosque was deemed small as many locals have to sit under hot environment as there aren't any roofs yet for mosque exterior. However, after few renovations such as the additions of roofs, the expansion of the mosque's spaces, and the additions of of fans for the exterior, the mosque have become one of important places for Muslim community in Jenderam Hilir.


Masjid Jameatus Solehah

Masjid Jameatus Solehah is a mosque located in Pekan Dengkil. Before the construction of Masjid As-Syakirin, locals in Jenderam Hilir will go to this mosque during Friday prayer

On the year 2020, some parts of the mosque are demolished as it is thought to be built on private-owned land that has been bought by construction developer to build new residential area.
