Local Food Eateries


Warung Aman Shah

Warung Aman Shah also known as Kedai Kurus is located at 6, Jalan Desa Sinaran 1, Kampung Tanjong, Dengkil, Selangor, Malaysia. This stall serves local Malaysian food such as Nasi Lemak, fried food, gravy food, sweet food and also biscuits that are ready to be packed in jars.

Service Option: Dine-in/Take away

Open Hours: Weekdays (6.00am-6.30pm) / Weekends (6.00am-11.00am)

Rating 4.5/5


Anjung Mesari

Anjung Mesari Restaurant provides a variety of breakfast and lunch dishes located at Jalan WP 2/1, Kampung Jenderam Hilir, 43800 Dengkil, Selangor. The restaurant claims that they also provide Latin American Restaurant themed food.

Service Option: Dine-in/Take away

Open Hours: Daily (7.00am-12.30am)

Rating 4.1/5



Lubook Makan King

Lubook Makan King is located at No 31 Kampung Bukit Piatus 43000 Dengkil, Selangor. They provide Malaysian community food with the theme of ‘makanan kampung’ such as Sotong Buncit, Gulai Kawah Kambing, Siput Sedut Masak Lemak, Daging, Masak Hitam, Patin Temerloh and Kerabu Perut Pucuk Paku

Service Option: Dine-in/Take away/Delivery Services (Foodpanda)

Open Hours: Open every day except Monday at 11.30am-4pm.

Rating 4.8/5






Kafe Kayu Dengkil

Kafe Kayu Restaurant serves a variety of delicious and appetizing village cuisine. Among them are Asam Pedas Set, Masak Lemak, Goreng Berempah, and Hot Soups. Different from other eateries, Kafe Kayu restaurant also provides services such as reservations and reservations for interested customers.

Service Option: Dine-in/Take away/Delivery Services (Foodpanda)

Open Hours: Open every day except Friday at 11am-5pm

Rating 4.8/5



PJP Cafe

PJP Cafe is a coffee shop located at 39, B18, Kampung Tanjong, 43800 Dengkil, Selangor. PJP Cafe provides a variety of food dishes for all customers who visit their restaurant.

Service Option: Dine-in

Open Hours: Open every day

Rating 4/5

Awe Taman

Kedai Makan Awe is located at Jalan Kajang - Dengkil, 43800 Selangor. Kedai Makan Awe primary category is American restaurants. If you are a person who likes seafood, steamboat & grill set, you can visit this one restaurant. Themed on Thai food, this Awe Taman also serves food such as hot dishes, soups, tom yam, and also western style.

Service Option: Dine-in/Take away

Open Hours: Daily at 5.30pm-12.00am.

Rating 3.1/5
